Type Information

This program uses 6 Pokémon types. Each type has a corresponding emoji, except the Normal type:
Pokémon Type Emoji
Fire 🔥
Grass 🌱
Water 💧
Electric ⚡
Rock 🗿
Normal No Emoji
Depending on the type of move you or your foe plays and the type of the Pokémon it is inflicted on, the attack can deal an increased amount of damage.
Attack Type Power Multiplier Pokémon Type
🔥 Fire + 10% 🌱 Grass
⚡ Electric + 10% 💧 Water
💧 Water + 10% 🔥 Fire
🌱 Grass + 10% 🗿 Rock
🗿 Rock + 10% ⚡ Electric
If the move is a Normal type, it will not deal an increased amount of damage.
